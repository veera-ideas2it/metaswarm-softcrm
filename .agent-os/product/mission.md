# Product Mission
# SoftCRM — Sales CRM for Software Companies

## What We're Building
SoftCRM is a production-ready sales pipeline CRM built specifically for
software and tech companies. It gives sales reps, managers, and founders
a structured way to manage their entire sales process — from first contact
to closed deal — with workflows that match how software is actually sold.

## The Problem We Solve
Software sales teams lose deals not because of bad salespeople, but because
of invisible gaps: no one knows a demo was missed, a technical evaluation
stalled, or a proposal went cold. Generic CRMs don't understand that
software sales has unique stages like Technical Validation and Security Review
that other industries don't have.

## Who Uses This
- **Sales Reps** — manage their own pipeline, log calls/emails/demos,
  move deals forward, track tasks
- **Sales Managers** — see their team's pipeline, spot stuck deals,
  track rep performance, forecast revenue
- **Founders / Admins** — configure pipeline stages, manage the team,
  view company-wide metrics

## Core Value Proposition
1. Pipeline built for software sales (not real estate or insurance)
2. Deal tracking with demo + technical evaluation stages baked in
3. Clear visibility into where every deal is stuck and why
4. Simple enough that reps actually use it daily

## What We Are NOT
- Not a marketing automation tool
- Not an email sending platform
- Not a customer support system
- Not trying to compete with HubSpot on features — we win on simplicity

## Success Metric for v1
A sales rep can open the app, see their full pipeline at a glance,
add a note on a deal, and move a deal to the next stage — all in under
60 seconds.
